About Us

The Tang Lab at Howard University investigates virus–host interactions that shape infection, immunity, and disease outcomes. We focus on Zika virus, Hepatitis C virus (HCV), Dengue virus, and human cytomegalovirus (HCMV), combining molecular virology, cell biology, and metabolic profiling to reveal how viruses rewire lipid and calcium homeostasis.
