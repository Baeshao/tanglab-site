Research Overview

Our work combines virology, lipidomics, and cellular metabolism to understand how viruses exploit host organelles and energy systems. We apply cardiac and neural organoid models, metabolic flux analysis, and genetic manipulation of viral BAC clones to dissect infection processes at subcellular resolution.

Projects

  • Zika Virus Cardiotropism: mapping calcium and lipid metabolism changes in cardiac organoids.
  • Dengue Virus Strain Diversity: comparing metabolic signatures across serotypes and host compartments.
  • HCMV Vector Design: engineering BAC-based vectors for antiviral and therapeutic gene delivery.
