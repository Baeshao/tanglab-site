Research Areas

  • Zika virus (ZIKV): molecular mechanisms of infection, lipid and calcium pathway modulation, and organoid model development (Ruth S. Cruz-Cosme & Shaokai Pei).
  • Dengue virus (DENV): replication dynamics and comparative host responses to ZIKV (Kaci Craft).
  • Hepatitis C virus (HCV): host–pathogen interactions, viral replication control, and antiviral drug evaluation (Imaan Muhammad).
  • Human cytomegalovirus (HCMV): BAC engineering, viral gene regulation, and vector development for mechanistic studies (Dr. Tang & team).
  • Shared themes: lipid metabolism, organelle communication, and host defense mechanisms as common determinants of viral pathogenesis.

Methodological Approaches

The lab employs an integrative toolkit including molecular cloning, quantitative PCR, viral BAC manipulation, immunofluorescence, subcellular fractionation, and organoid modeling. Metabolic assays and calcium imaging are used to dissect host responses to viral infection at subcellular and systems levels.
